Why Families Choose Us

The Children's School — The Difference

Discover why families across Atlanta trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our faculty at The Children's School are highly qualified, experienced, and passionate about teaching and learning. They are dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

📚

Small Class Sizes

Our small class sizes allow for personalized attention, one-on-one instruction, and a more immersive learning experience, ensuring that every child receives the individual support they need to succeed.

🏞️

Safe Campus

Our campus is a secure and welcoming environment, designed to promote a sense of community and belonging among our students, faculty, and staff.

📊

Balanced Curriculum

Our curriculum at The Children's School is designed to provide a balanced and well-rounded education, incorporating a range of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, science, social studies, music, art, and physical education.

🌟

Whole-Child Approach

We believe in fostering the whole child – academically, socially, emotionally, and physically – and provide opportunities for our students to engage in extracurricular activities, community service, and character development.

👫

Strong Community

Our community at The Children's School is warm, welcoming, and inclusive, providing a sense of belonging and connection among our students, faculty, and staff.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-4, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development through play-based learning and hands-on activities.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-4, emphasizing a balanced curriculum that includes language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, as well as music, art, and physical education.

🎓

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students in grades 5-8, focusing on academic rigor, character development, and community service, as well as a range of extracurricular activities and sports teams.
