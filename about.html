Our Mission & History

With over 20 years of experience in Atlanta's private school scene, The Children's School has established itself as a leader in providing a well-rounded education that prepares children for success in an ever-changing world. Our experienced faculty and staff are dedicated to creating a supportive and engaging environment that encourages children to explore, discover, and grow.

At The Children's School, we believe that learning should be a joyful and interactive experience. Our teaching approach is designed to be student-centered, with a focus on hands-on learning, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to meet the needs of each child, with a focus on STEM, arts, and character education.

Beyond the classroom, The Children's School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities and clubs that allow children to explore their passions and interests. From sports and music to art and drama, we believe that every child should have the opportunity to grow and develop in a supportive and inclusive community.
