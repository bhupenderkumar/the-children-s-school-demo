From Our Head of School

As the principal of The Children's School, I am committed to creating a community where every child feels seen, heard, and valued. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to provide a rich and engaging educational experience that prepares our students for success in all areas of life.

Our Mission & History

Founded in 1968, The Children's School has a long history of providing a high-quality education to children in Atlanta. Our school is committed to fostering a love of learning and a sense of community that extends beyond the classroom.

Our teaching approach is centered around project-based learning, which encourages students to think critically and creatively as they explore a range of subjects. We also prioritize character development, teaching our students important life skills such as empathy, self-awareness, and responsibility.

At The Children's School, we believe that a well-rounded education includes opportunities for physical activity, arts, and community service. Our students have access to a range of extracurricular activities, including sports teams, music programs, and volunteer opportunities.
