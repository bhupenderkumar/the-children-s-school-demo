Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-4, and is focused on developing social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing and play-based environment.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves children in grades K-4, and is designed to promote a love of learning, creativity, and critical thinking in a supportive and inclusive environment.

🔬

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves children in grades 5-8, and is designed to prepare students for high school and beyond, with a focus on academic rigor, character development, and leadership skills.

🔍

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to promote problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity in science, technology, engineering, and math.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program is designed to promote creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts in music, drama, and visual arts.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program is designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship in a variety of sports and activities.
